WHAT IS IT?

I created a DC motor that utilizes all the skills and knowledge during my physics course for the final project. DC motors are used in our everyday lives and their primary function is to convert electrical energy to rotational kinetic energy. This is what powers your fan, cars, and toys.

What seperates DC motors from AC motors is that DC is "direct current", a steady stream of current as opposed to "alternating current", which alternates.

The project combines ideas of electromagnetism, circuits, and motors.

WHAT I LEARNED

This was also my first hands on project in High School due to the difficulties of the pandemic.
