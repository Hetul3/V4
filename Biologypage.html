SBI3U

Unit 1: Evolution

The progress on the theory of Evolution. Evidence for evolution and patterns of evolution. Earth's eras and human evolution.

Unit 2: Genetics P1

Asexual and sexual reproduction. Cloning and genetic technologies. Mitosis and meiosis, non-disjunction syndromes. The DNA structure and DNA replication.

Unit 3: Genetics P2

Mendelian genetics, determining the genotype and phenotype. Punnet squares, dihybrids, pedigrees. Dominant, incomplete dominance, co-dominance.

Unit 4: Circulatory System

Blood vessels and blood properties in the circulatory system. The heart and other organs involved, the lymphatic system.

Unit 5: Respiratory System

Respiratory organs, functions, and importance. Gas exchange, partial pressures, respiratory system of other organisms, gills, and partial pressures.

Unit 6: Digestive System

Flowchart, enzymes and secretions for the digestion of nutrients. Main and accessory organs that aid in the digestive process.

SPH3U

Unit 1: Kinematics

The five kinematic equations and how to utilize them. Velocity-time graphs, distance-time graphs, acceleration-time graphs.

Unit 2: Forces

Newtonian Forces and calculating the different forces acting on an object. Gravitational forces and Free-Body Diagrams.

Unit 3: Circuits

The idea of electricity, AC vs DC current voltage, current, resistence. Electrical formulas and circuit diagrams.

Unit 4: Electromagnetism

Electromagnetic waves, the right hand rules for forces of attraction and polarity. Lorentz force and electromagnets.

Unit 5: Waves and Sound

Wave structure, frequency and period calculations, speed of sound calculations. The doppler effect, constructive and destructive interference, harmonics, nodes and antinodes.

Unit 2: Forces

Force diagrams, Newtonian physics, third law pairs, pulleys and tension, gravitational force, centripital acceleration.

Unit 3: Energy and Momentum

Types of energy, work, Hooke's law, momentum, law of conservation of momentum, 2D momentum.

Unit 4: Fields

Electric field, electric force, magnetic fields, magnetic forces.
